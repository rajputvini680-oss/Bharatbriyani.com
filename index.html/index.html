About Us

Located in the vibrant heart of Motijheel, Bharat Biryani is a cherished culinary haven where tradition meets innovation. Known for its commitment to quality ingredients, delightful flavors, and exceptional service.

Nestled near Ners Shyam Cinema Hall in Motijheel, Bharat Biryani is a convenient stop for both locals and visitors exploring the city. The restaurant's interior is elegantly designed to provide a cozy yet refined ambiance.
